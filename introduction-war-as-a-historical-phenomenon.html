Introduction: War as a Historical Phenomenon

Shtan Bohdan 29/04/2026

The Russo-Ukrainian War, which unfolded in 2014 and entered its full-scale phase in 2022, is often perceived as a sudden conflict. However, a deeper historical analysis shows that it has a long-standing background.

Relations between Ukraine and Russia have been shaped over centuries and were accompanied by a struggle for political independence, cultural identity, and the right to the existence of Ukrainian statehood. Therefore, it is appropriate to view the modern war not as an isolated event, but as a continuation of historical processes.
